Frequently Asked Questions

Is everything on NextGen Library really free?
Yes, absolutely. All study materials, articles, notes, and tools on NextGen Digital Library are completely free to access. There are no hidden fees, subscriptions, or paywalls. We believe education should be accessible to everyone.
Can I request specific study material for my course?
Absolutely! Use the contact form above and select "Resource Request" as the subject. Tell us your course, semester, university, and the specific topic you need. We'll do our best to add it to our platform.
How often is new content added?
We add new blog articles and study materials every week. The blog is regularly updated with new educational content on programming, career guidance, exam tips, and technology topics relevant to CS/IT students.
Can I contribute content or notes to the platform?
Yes! If you have high-quality notes, guides, or articles that you'd like to share with the NextGen community, please reach out via the contact form. We welcome contributions from students and educators excited about education.
Does NextGen Library work offline?
Yes! NextGen Digital Library is a Progressive Web App (PWA). You can install it on your phone or laptop and access many features even without an internet connection, making it perfect for students who don't always have reliable connectivity.
